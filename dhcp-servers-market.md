<p><strong>The DHCP Servers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UDP Networks</li><li>IP Networks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DHCP servers operate within two main market types: UDP networks and IP networks. In UDP networks, DHCP facilitates dynamic IP address allocation, allowing devices to seamlessly connect and communicate without manual configuration. This is crucial for environments like home networks or public Wi-Fi. Conversely, in IP networks, DHCP servers manage a larger pool of IP addresses for enterprise environments, ensuring efficient network management, device mobility, and reducing administrative overhead. Both types enhance network efficiency and user experience.</p></p>
