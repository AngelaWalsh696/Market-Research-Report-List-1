<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Roller Compactor [Road Roller] Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Drum Vibratory Roller</li><li>Double Drum Vibratory Roller</li><li>Tire Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roller compactor market is segmented into three main types: single drum vibratory rollers, double drum vibratory rollers, and tire rollers. Single drum vibratory rollers use a single heavy drum to compact soil, asphalt, or gravel, excelling in high-density applications. Double drum vibratory rollers feature two drums for even compaction, ideal for asphalt surfaces. Tire rollers, equipped with rubber tires, provide flexibility and are suitable for finishing work, offering a smoother surface while reducing the risk of damage from compaction.</p></p>
