<p><p>The hydrogen cylinder market is categorized into five types based on construction materials. Type I cylinders are made from steel or aluminum and are the simplest and heaviest. Type II cylinders have a metal liner with composite wrap, reducing weight. Type III cylinders feature a composite shell with a thin metal liner, further decreasing weight. Type IV cylinders are fully composite with a plastic liner, offering the lightest option. Type V cylinders are emerging designs combining advanced materials for enhanced performance and safety.</p></p>
