<p><p>The air ultrasonic sensors market is divided into high-frequency and low-frequency sensor types. High-frequency sensors operate at elevated frequencies, often above 40 kHz, offering precise detection and measurement capabilities suited for short-range applications. They are typically used in proximity sensing and automation. In contrast, low-frequency sensors function below 40 kHz and are ideal for longer-range applications, such as distance measurement and obstacle detection. These sensors find applications in various industries, including automotive, robotics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
