<p><p>The sealing gasket market is divided into two primary types: metallic and non-metallic gaskets. Metallic gaskets, made from materials like steel or copper, are designed for high-temperature and high-pressure applications, offering durability and resilience. Non-metallic gaskets, typically composed of rubber, foam, or certain plastics, provide flexibility and are ideal for low-pressure applications or situations requiring chemical resistance. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring leak-proof seals in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
