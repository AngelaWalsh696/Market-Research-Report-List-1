<p><p>The medical resuscitation equipment market comprises several key types. Cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) machines provide automated chest compressions during cardiac arrest, enhancing the effectiveness of CPR. Defibrillators deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm in cases of arrhythmias. Ventilators assist or replace spontaneous breathing for patients unable to breathe effectively. Other devices may include airway management tools and suction equipment. Together, these products play a crucial role in emergency medical settings to revive and stabilize patients.</p></p>
