<p><ul><li>Single Entry Racks</li><li>Double Entry Racks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Drive-in Rack market consists of two primary types: Single Entry Racks and Double Entry Racks. Single Entry Racks allow access to pallets from one side, maximizing space efficiency and ideal for first-in, first-out (FIFO) stock management. In contrast, Double Entry Racks enable access from both sides, facilitating quicker inventory retrieval and higher storage density, suitable for last-in, first-out (LIFO) systems. Both types cater to various warehouse configurations and operational needs, enhancing storage optimization and workflow efficiency.</p></p>
