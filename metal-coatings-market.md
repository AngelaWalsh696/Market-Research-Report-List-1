<p><p>The metal coatings market is primarily divided into two types: liquid coatings and powder coatings. Liquid coatings involve applying a wet paint that contains solvents and pigments, offering good adhesion and flexibility. They are commonly used in various applications for their smooth finish and ease of application. On the other hand, powder coatings consist of dry particles that are electrostatically applied and then cured, resulting in a durable, thick finish that is more environmentally friendly and resistant to chipping and fading.</p></p>
