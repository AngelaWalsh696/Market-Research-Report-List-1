<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing encompasses various techniques, primarily Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powders layer by layer, resulting in complex geometries and high-density parts. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to achieve similar results, particularly with high-melting-point metals. Other methods in this market may include binder jetting and direct energy deposition, each offering unique advantages for different applications in industries like aerospace and medical.</p></p>
