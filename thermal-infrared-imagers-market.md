<p><p>The thermal infrared imagers market is categorized into uncooled and cooled thermal imaging imagers. Uncooled thermal imagers utilize detectors that operate at ambient temperatures, offering advantages like compact size and lower power consumption, making them ideal for various applications such as building inspections and security. In contrast, cooled thermal imagers feature detectors maintained at cryogenic temperatures, providing superior sensitivity and image quality. These are commonly used in demanding environments like military and aerospace applications where precision is crucial.</p></p>
