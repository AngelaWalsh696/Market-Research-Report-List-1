<p><p>The optical distance sensors market primarily comprises two types: laser-based sensors and LED-based sensors. Laser type sensors utilize focused laser beams to measure distances with high precision, making them ideal for long-range applications. They are widely used in industrial automation and robotics. On the other hand, LED type sensors use light-emitting diodes to detect distances, often employed in shorter range applications due to their simpler design and cost-effectiveness. Both types are crucial for enhancing automation and precision in various industries.</p></p>
