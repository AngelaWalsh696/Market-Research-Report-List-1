<p><p>The Smart Parking Sensors market includes technologies like Electromagnetic Parking Sensors and Ultrasonic Reversing Sensors. Electromagnetic sensors detect vehicles or obstacles using magnetic fields, offering high accuracy and reliability, especially in harsh weather conditions. Ultrasonic Reversing Sensors use sound waves to measure distances, providing real-time alerts to drivers during parking. Both types enhance vehicle safety and convenience, with electromagnetic sensors excelling in precision and ultrasonic sensors being cost-effective and widely used in automotive applications.</p></p>
