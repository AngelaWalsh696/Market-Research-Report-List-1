<p><p>The CO2 stunning system market is primarily categorized into Continuous Gas Systems and Disposable Gas Systems. Continuous Gas Systems provide a constant flow of CO2, ensuring a steady and controlled stunning process for livestock during slaughter. In contrast, Disposable Gas Systems use pre-packaged canisters of CO2, offering a more convenient and portable option. Both types focus on humane treatment by minimizing stress and pain, but they cater to different operational needs and facility requirements in the meat processing industry.</p></p>
