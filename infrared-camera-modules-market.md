<p><strong>The Infrared Camera Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shortwave</li><li>Medium Wave</li><li>Long Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared camera modules market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: shortwave, medium wave, and long wave. Shortwave infrared (SWIR) cameras operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers range, ideal for applications like solar cell inspection and surveillance. Medium wave infrared (MWIR) cameras, typically ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are commonly used in military and industrial settings. Long wave infrared (LWIR) cameras, spanning 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in thermal imaging for building inspections and night vision applications. Each type offers distinct advantages tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
