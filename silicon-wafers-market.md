<p><strong>The Silicon Wafers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>N-type</li><li>P-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon wafers are categorized into N-type and P-type based on their doping elements. N-type wafers, doped with elements like phosphorus, have excess electrons, providing enhanced electrical conductivity. They are commonly used in modern electronics due to their higher efficiency in solar cells and semiconductor devices. Conversely, P-type wafers, doped with elements such as boron, create "holes" or positive charge carriers. Together, these wafer types are integral to the semiconductor industry, impacting performance and application across various technologies.</p></p>
