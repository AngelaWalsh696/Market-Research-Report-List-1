<p><p>Castration-resistant prostate cancer (CRPC) treatment options encompass various approaches. Hormonal therapy aims to lower testosterone levels. Other treatments may include immunotherapy, targeted therapies, or clinical trials, offering patients personalized care options in managing CRPC effectively.</p></p>
