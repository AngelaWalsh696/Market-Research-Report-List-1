<p><ul><li>Side Wheel Motors</li><li>Center Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrically trikes market is categorized primarily into side wheel motors and center motors. Side wheel motors are installed on the rear wheels, providing enhanced stability and traction, making them suitable for uneven terrains. They offer a balanced weight distribution and can accommodate greater payloads. In contrast, center motors are positioned centrally, typically near the bottom bracket, delivering efficient power with a compact design. This setup allows for better handling and agility, ideal for urban commuting and recreational use.</p></p>
