<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel motor market primarily consists of two types: inner-rotor motors and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors have the rotor located inside the stator, offering compact designs and efficient performance, making them suitable for applications requiring space efficiency. In contrast, external-rotor motors feature the rotor placed outside the stator, allowing for better heat dissipation and higher torque outputs. Both types cater to the growing demand for electric vehicles, providing unique advantages in power delivery and design flexibility.</p></p>
