<p><p>The FEP tubing material market encompasses two primary types: heat shrinkable tubing and extruded tubing. Heat shrinkable tubing is designed to shrink upon exposure to heat, providing a tight protective seal around wires and components, ideal for insulation and environmental resistance. Extruded tubing, on the other hand, is manufactured through a continuous process to create uniform shapes and sizes. It offers flexibility and is commonly used in applications requiring chemical resistance and durability, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
