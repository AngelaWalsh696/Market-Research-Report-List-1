<p><p>The cell harvesting device market encompasses various types, each serving unique purposes in cell collection. Umbilical cord harvesting focuses on extracting stem cells from the umbilical cord post-birth, crucial for regenerative medicine. Bone marrow harvesting involves obtaining stem cells from the bone marrow, essential for treatments like transplants. Peripheral blood harvesting collects stem cells from circulating blood, often through apheresis. Lastly, adipose tissue harvesting gathers stem cells from fatty tissue, gaining traction for cosmetic and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
