<p><ul><li>Ileostomy</li><li>Colostomy</li><li>Urostomy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ostomy care solutions encompass products designed for patients with surgical openings in the abdominal wall, categorized into three main types: ileostomy, colostomy, and urostomy. Ileostomy involves the removal of the ileum, requiring specialized pouches to manage waste. Colostomy, stemming from the colon, similarly uses pouches but may differ in design based on the stoma’s location. Urostomy is for urinary diversion, needing pouches to collect urine. Each market segment focuses on addressing distinct patient needs and improving quality of life.</p></p>
