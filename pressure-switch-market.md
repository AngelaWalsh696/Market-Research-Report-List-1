<p><ul><li>Electromechanical</li><li>Solid-State</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure switch market is primarily divided into two types: electromechanical and solid-state switches. Electromechanical pressure switches utilize mechanical components, such as diaphragms or pistons, to detect pressure changes, often providing reliable performance in industrial applications. In contrast, solid-state pressure switches employ electronic components, offering faster response times and higher durability, as they have no moving parts. Both types serve diverse industries, including HVAC, automotive, and manufacturing, catering to various pressure sensing needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
