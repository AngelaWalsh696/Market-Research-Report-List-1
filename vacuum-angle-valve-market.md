<p><p>The vacuum angle valve market is segmented into three main types: manual angle valves, pneumatic angle valves, and electromagnetic angle valves. Manual angle valves require human operation to control flow, offering simplicity and reliability. Pneumatic angle valves utilize compressed air for automatic operation, ideal for high-speed applications. Electromagnetic angle valves employ electrical signals to regulate flow, providing precise control and rapid response. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing efficiency in vacuum systems across various applications.</p></p>
