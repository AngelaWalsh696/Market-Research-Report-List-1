<p><p>The fiber optic mechanical splices market offers various coupling types suited for connecting optical fibers. Flexible couplings allow for movement and adjustability, enhancing alignment. Swivel couplings enable rotation between fibers, reducing stress and improving performance. Capillary couplings utilize a glass tube for precise alignment, ensuring minimal signal loss. V-groove couplings provide stability by securely holding fibers in place. Other types may include specialized or innovative designs catering to specific applications, enhancing reliability and efficiency in fiber optic networks.</p></p>
