<p><strong>The Submarine Pipeline Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine pipeline market features three main types of pipes: Electric Resistance Welded (ERW) pipes, Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded (LSAW) pipes, and Spiral Submerged Arc Welded (SSAW) pipes. ERW pipes are known for their strength and are produced by welding coils of steel together. LSAW pipes have a longitudinal seam and are used for high-pressure applications. SSAW pipes are manufactured by spirally welding steel strips, offering increased flexibility and resistance to deformation, making them suitable for various underwater applications.</p></p>
