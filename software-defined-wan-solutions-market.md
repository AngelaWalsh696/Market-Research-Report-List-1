<p><p>Software-Defined WAN (SD-WAN) solutions can be categorized into three main types: virtual appliances, physical appliances, and hybrid models. Virtual appliances are software-based solutions that run on existing hardware, offering scalability and flexibility. Physical appliances are dedicated hardware devices that deliver robust performance and reliability for WAN connectivity. Hybrid solutions combine both virtual and physical appliances, allowing organizations to leverage the advantages of both approaches, offering a balance of performance, scalability, and ease of management in diverse network environments.</p></p>
