<p><p>The 5G network infrastructure market comprises various cell types that cater to different coverage needs. **Femto cells** are small, low-power base stations designed for indoor use, enhancing coverage in homes or small offices. **Pico cells** offer a slightly larger range, suitable for places like shopping malls or airports. **Micro cells** cover larger urban areas, filling in gaps in macro networks. **Macro cells**, the largest of the four, provide extensive coverage for wide areas and serve as the backbone of 5G networks.</p></p>
