<p><p>The street light controllers market is primarily divided into two types: analog and digital. Analog type controllers utilize traditional circuitry to manage lighting, often relying on simple timers and sensors for operations. In contrast, digital type controllers employ advanced microprocessors and software algorithms, enabling more precise control, remote management, and integration with smart city technologies. Digital controllers typically offer enhanced features such as energy optimization, real-time monitoring, and adaptability to varying lighting conditions, making them increasingly popular in modern applications.</p></p>
