<p><p>The medical sterilization system market comprises various types of sterilization equipment. Heat sterilization equipment utilizes high temperatures to eliminate microorganisms, making it effective for surgical instruments. Low temperature sterilization equipment employs methods like ethylene oxide or hydrogen peroxide vapor for sensitive devices that cannot withstand high heat. Radiation sterilization equipment uses gamma rays or electron beams to sterilize medical products without direct contact. The “other” category includes emerging technologies and hybrid methods that combine various sterilization techniques for enhanced efficacy.</p></p>
