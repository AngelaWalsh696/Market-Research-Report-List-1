<p><p>Biological treatment equipment market can be categorized into dry fermentation and wet fermentation types. Dry fermentation involves the anaerobic digestion of organic materials in a solid-state, generating biogas and organic fertilizers with minimal water use. This method is efficient for managing waste with low moisture content. In contrast, wet fermentation utilizes a slurry of organic matter mixed with water, facilitating microorganisms to break down waste in a liquid environment, producing biogas and digestate. Each type serves specific waste treatment needs and applications.</p></p>
