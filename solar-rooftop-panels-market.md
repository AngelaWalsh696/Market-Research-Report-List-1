<p><p>The solar rooftop panels market is divided into two primary types: on-grid and off-grid systems. On-grid systems are connected to the public electricity grid, allowing homeowners to sell excess energy back to utilities and reduce electricity bills. In contrast, off-grid systems operate independently from the grid, utilizing battery storage to provide power for households without access to grid electricity. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with on-grid systems focusing on cost-efficiency and off-grid systems emphasizing energy independence.</p></p>
