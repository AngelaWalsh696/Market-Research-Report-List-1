<p><strong>The Medical Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Solids & Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical adhesive market is categorized into four main types: water-based, solvent-based, solids, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives use water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Solvent-based adhesives contain volatile organic compounds, providing strong bonding but may have safety concerns. Solids refer to adhesives in a non-liquid form, often used in specific applications. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that bond upon cooling, offering fast setting times and versatility for various medical applications.</p></p>
