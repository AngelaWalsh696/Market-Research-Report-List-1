<p><p>The Emergency Transport Ventilators market is categorized into three main types. Electronic ventilators utilize advanced electronics for precise control over ventilation parameters and are often more portable. Pneumatic ventilators operate mechanically using gas pressure, making them reliable and easy to use in emergencies. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine both technologies, offering the reliability of pneumatic systems with the flexibility and control of electronic ones. Each type serves critical roles in delivering respiratory support during transport of patients in critical conditions.</p></p>
