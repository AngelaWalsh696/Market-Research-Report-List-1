<p><ul><li>Single Site</li><li>Dual Site</li><li>Four Sites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pick-and-place handlers are categorized into three market types based on their operational capabilities: single site, dual site, and four site. Single site handlers operate in one location, ideal for smaller-scale operations. Dual site handlers enhance productivity by managing tasks at two locations simultaneously, promoting efficiency. Four site handlers are designed for high-capacity operations, handling tasks across four locations concurrently, making them suitable for large-scale production environments. Each type offers varying levels of flexibility and efficiency to meet different operational needs.</p></p>
