<p><p>College and university management software is categorized primarily into two market types: on-cloud and on-premise solutions. On-cloud software is hosted on remote servers, allowing institutions to access the system via the internet, promoting scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance costs. Conversely, on-premise software is installed locally on the institution's servers, providing enhanced control and data security but often requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Each option offers distinct advantages, catering to different institutional needs and technological capabilities.</p></p>
