<p><p>The flexible pipes market in the oil and gas industry encompasses various types, including risers, flowlines, and other applications. Risers are used to transport hydrocarbons from the seabed to surface facilities, while flowlines are designed for the movement of fluids over distances on the ocean floor. These solutions offer advantages like ease of installation, reduced weight, and enhanced flexibility, making them ideal for challenging environments.</p></p>
