<p><strong>The Acrylic Elastomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ACM</li><li>AEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic elastomer market is primarily categorized into two types: ACM (Acrylic Rubber) and AEM (Acrylic Copolymer Rubber). ACM is known for its excellent heat, oil, and weather resistance, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. AEM, on the other hand, offers enhanced mechanical properties and superior ozone resistance, often used in automotive seals and gaskets. Both types are leveraged for their durability and performance in challenging environments, contributing to growth in industries like automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
