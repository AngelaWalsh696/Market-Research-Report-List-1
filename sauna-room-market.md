<p><p>The sauna room market encompasses two primary types: infrared saunas and conventional saunas. Infrared saunas utilize infrared heaters to emit radiant heat that directly warms the body, promoting relaxation and detoxification at lower temperatures. In contrast, conventional saunas use traditional methods such as electric or wood-burning stoves to heat the air, creating a hot and humid environment. Both types offer health benefits and cater to different consumer preferences, driving growth in the wellness and fitness sectors.</p></p>
