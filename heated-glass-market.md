<p><p>The heated glass market primarily includes laminated glass and hollow glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more glass layers bonded with a plastic interlayer, providing safety and sound insulation, making it ideal for architecture and automotive applications. Hollow glass, characterized by its sealed air or gas-filled space, offers excellent thermal insulation and lightweight properties. Both types serve diverse industries, such as construction, automotive, and furniture, enhancing energy efficiency and safety in various applications.</p></p>
