<p><p>The mild steel wire market is categorized into two primary types: no cover and galvanized coated. No cover mild steel wire consists of untreated wire that offers high strength and weldability but is vulnerable to rust and corrosion. In contrast, galvanized coated wire is coated with a layer of zinc, providing enhanced corrosion resistance and durability, making it ideal for outdoor applications. Both types are widely used in construction, fencing, and various industrial applications due to their cost-effectiveness and versatility.</p></p>
