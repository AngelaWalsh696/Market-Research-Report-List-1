<p><strong>The Amidated Low Sugar Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amidated Low Sugar Pectin (ALSP) is categorized into two main types based on methoxyl content: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin. HM Pectin requires a higher sugar content and is ideal for traditional jams and jellies, providing a strong gelling ability at high sugar concentrations. In contrast, LM Pectin functions effectively with lower sugar levels and is suitable for reduced-sugar products. It utilizes calcium ions for gel formation, catering to health-conscious consumers seeking alternatives with less sugar.</p></p>
