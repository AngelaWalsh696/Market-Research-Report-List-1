<p><p>The air-defense missile system market comprises various types, primarily including Man-Portable Air-Defense Systems (MANPADS) and Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems. In contrast, Stationary or Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems are larger, fixed installations or vehicle-mounted systems, offering comprehensive air defense capabilities over wider areas. Both types play crucial roles in modern military operations, enhancing protection against aerial threats across diverse environments and operational scenarios.</p></p>
