<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The range finder market is primarily divided into two types: laser and ultrasonic. Laser range finders use light waves to accurately measure distances, often preferred for outdoor applications due to their precision and long-range capabilities. Ultrasonic range finders, on the other hand, utilize sound waves to gauge distances, suitable for shorter ranges and indoor environments. Each type caters to specific needs, with laser models excelling in distance and accuracy, while ultrasonic models offer cost-effectiveness and ease of use in confined spaces.</p></p>
