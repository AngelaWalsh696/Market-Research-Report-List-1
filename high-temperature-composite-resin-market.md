<p><p>The high-temperature composite resin market encompasses various types including phenolic, epoxy, thermoplastic, polyimide, and cyanate ester resins. Phenolic resins offer excellent heat resistance and low smoke generation, making them suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. Epoxy resins provide strong mechanical properties and good chemical resistance. Thermoplastic resins offer flexibility and recyclability. Polyimide resins excel in extreme thermal environments, while cyanate esters stand out for their low thermal expansion and high-temperature performance, catering to advanced industries like aerospace and electronics.</p></p>
