<p><p>The spine implant devices market encompasses various types, including spinal fusion and fixation, which stabilize the spine by joining vertebrae. Vertebral compression fracture treatment focuses on repairing fractured vertebrae, often using kyphoplasty or vertebroplasty. Non-fusion devices aim to stabilize the spine without permanently joining bones, maintaining natural movement. Motion preservation technologies, such as artificial discs, enable normal spinal function while addressing pain, providing alternatives to traditional fusion methods and enhancing patient mobility and quality of life.</p></p>
