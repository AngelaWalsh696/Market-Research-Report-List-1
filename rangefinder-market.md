<p><strong>The Rangefinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Rangefinder</li><li>Acoustic Rangefinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rangefinder market consists primarily of two types: photoelectric and acoustic rangefinders. Photoelectric rangefinders utilize light waves, such as laser technology, to measure distances by calculating the time it takes for a light pulse to return after reflecting off an object. In contrast, acoustic rangefinders employ sound waves, commonly used in applications like underwater measurements and surveying, measuring distances based on the speed of sound in the medium. Each type serves distinct industries, including surveying, construction, and maritime navigation.</p></p>
