<p><strong>The MEMS Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kelvin Testing</li><li>Four-probe Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS testing market encompasses various techniques to evaluate the performance and reliability of Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems. Kelvin testing is a precise method for measuring low-resistance pathways, minimizing voltage drop and ensuring accurate current measurements, vital for assessing MEMS device quality. In contrast, four-probe testing employs four distinct electrodes to determine sample resistivity, providing a non-destructive evaluation method for thin films and materials. Both techniques are crucial for ensuring the operational integrity of MEMS components in various applications.</p></p>
