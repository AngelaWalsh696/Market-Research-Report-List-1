<p><strong>The Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED displays market is primarily divided into two types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED displays utilize a backplane of thin-film transistors to control individual pixels, enabling higher resolution and faster refresh rates, making them ideal for smartphones and TVs. In contrast, PMOLED technology employs a simpler design with rows and columns of pixels turned on sequentially, suitable for smaller screens and lower-power applications, such as wearables and simple displays.</p></p>
