<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) encompasses various inspection methods used to assess the integrity of materials without causing damage. Magnetic Particle Testing uses magnetic fields to reveal surface and near-surface defects. Liquid Penetrant Testing identifies flaws on non-porous materials using dye.
