<p><p>Collision Avoidance Technology encompasses various safety systems designed to prevent accidents in vehicles. Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) maintains safe distances from other vehicles by adjusting speed. Blind Spot Detection (BSD) alerts drivers about vehicles in their blind spots. Forward Collision Warning (FCW) detects potential collisions and warns the driver, while Lane Departure Warning Systems (LDWS) monitor lane positioning and alert drivers if they drift without signaling. Together, these technologies enhance road safety and reduce the likelihood of accidents.</p></p>
