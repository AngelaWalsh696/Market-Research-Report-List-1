<p><strong>The Tinning Lines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-dip Tinning Line</li><li>Electrolytic Tinning Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tinning lines market comprises two main types: hot-dip tinning lines and electrolytic tinning lines. Hot-dip tinning involves immersing steel in molten tin, providing robust corrosion resistance and a durable finish, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, electrolytic tinning uses an electrochemical process to deposit tin onto steel, resulting in a thinner layer with a smoother finish, suitable for applications where a delicate, uniform coating is critical. Both processes cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing product longevity.</p></p>
