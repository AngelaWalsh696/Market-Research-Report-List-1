<p><strong>The Air Diffusers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porous Plate Type</li><li>Porous Tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air diffusers market comprises various types, including porous plate and porous tube diffusers. Porous plate diffusers feature a flat surface with tiny holes, allowing for even air distribution across large areas, making them ideal for shallow water applications. In contrast, porous tube diffusers consist of elongated tubes with fine pores, providing efficient aeration in deeper waters. Both types enhance oxygen transfer and promote efficient mixing in environments like wastewater treatment, aquaculture, and industrial processes, contributing to improved operational efficiency.</p></p>
