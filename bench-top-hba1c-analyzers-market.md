<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Immunoassay</li><li>Boronate Affinity Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bench-top HbA1c analyzers are vital in diabetes management, facilitating rapid and accurate glycemic control assessments. The market contains three main types: </p><p>1. **Ion Exchange Chromatography** is a gold standard methodology that separates HbA1c from other hemoglobin variants, providing precise measurements.</p><p>2. **Immunoassay** utilizes antibodies to quantify HbA1c levels, offering high sensitivity and specificity.</p><p>3. **Boronate Affinity Technology** relies on the binding of HbA1c to boronic acid, enabling quick and efficient detection. These technologies cater to varying laboratory needs and enhance patient care.</p></p>
