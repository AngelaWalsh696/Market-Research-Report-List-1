<p><strong>The Optical Polyester Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Super-Thin Film (below 6μm)</li><li>Universal Film (6-65μm)</li><li>Thick Film (above 65μm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical polyester film market is categorized into three primary types based on thickness. Super-thin films, measuring below 6μm, are ideal for applications requiring minimal weight and flexibility, such as displays and packaging. Universal films, ranging from 6 to 65μm, offer a balance of performance and versatility, commonly used in electronics and automotive sectors. Thick films, exceeding 65μm, provide enhanced durability and are suitable for protective applications in industrial contexts, ensuring optimal performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
