<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The replacement tire market primarily consists of radial and bias tires, each serving different performance needs. Radial tires feature steel belts that enhance durability, fuel efficiency, and handling, making them suitable for modern vehicles. In contrast, bias tires have layers of fabric that run diagonally, offering a flexible sidewall and improved load capacity, often preferred in agricultural and heavy-duty applications. Understanding the distinctions between these types allows consumers to make informed choices based on vehicle requirements and driving conditions.</p></p>
