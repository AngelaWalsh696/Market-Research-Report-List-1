<p><p>The automobile rainfall sensor market includes various types designed to detect and respond to rainfall. Flow sensors measure the rate of water flow to activate windshield wipers. Electrostatic sensors detect changes in the electrical charge caused by raindrops. Piezoelectric sensors generate an electric charge in response to pressure from raindrops. Infrared sensors use light waves to sense moisture levels. Other types may include mechanical or advanced digital sensors, catering to different vehicle designs and requirements for enhanced safety and convenience.</p></p>
