<p><p>Flame Ionization Detectors (FIDs) are analytical instruments commonly used in gas chromatography to detect organic compounds. They operate by burning the sample gas in a hydrogen flame, which produces ions; these ions generate a measurable current, indicating the concentration of the analytes. FIDs are highly sensitive and are particularly effective for hydrocarbons, making them essential in environmental monitoring, petrochemical industries, and food safety testing.</p><p>The Flame Ionization Detectors Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, driven by increasing demand for effective monitoring of air pollution and regulations regarding emissions.
