<p><p>The Catering Hi-lift market is segmented into two main types: Wide Body and Narrow Body. Wide Body Hi-lifts are designed for larger aircraft, facilitating high-capacity meal service, while Narrow Body Hi-lifts cater to smaller aircraft, focusing on efficiency and quick service. Wide Body models typically offer greater storage and handling capacities, essential for long-haul flights, whereas Narrow Body models prioritize compactness and agility, ideal for shorter routes where space is limited. Both serve vital roles in the aviation catering industry.</p></p>
