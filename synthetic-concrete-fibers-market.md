<p><p>The synthetic concrete fibers market is divided into two main types: macro-synthetic fibers and micro-synthetic fibers. Macro-synthetic fibers are larger in diameter and are primarily used to enhance the structural integrity and durability of concrete, providing resistance to cracking and improving load distribution. In contrast, micro-synthetic fibers are smaller and mainly serve to control plastic shrinkage cracking and improve the toughness of concrete. Both types play crucial roles in various construction applications, enhancing performance and longevity.</p></p>
