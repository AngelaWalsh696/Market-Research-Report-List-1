<p><p>The Polyethylene (PE) Wax market comprises three main types: Polymerized PE Wax, Modified PE Wax, and Cracked PE Wax. Polymerized PE Wax, created through polymerization processes, offers high purity and consistency, making it suitable for applications such as coatings and additives. Modified PE Wax undergoes chemical changes to enhance properties like adhesion and dispersibility, commonly used in inks and adhesives. Cracked PE Wax is derived from cracking heavy hydrocarbon feedstocks, providing lower molecular weight options ideal for lubricants and surfactants.</p></p>
