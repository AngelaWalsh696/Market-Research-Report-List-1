<p><p>The pallet truck market is primarily categorized into manual and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks require operators to pump a handle to raise and lower loads, making them cost-effective and suitable for lighter tasks in smaller spaces. In contrast, electric pallet trucks utilize battery power for lifting and maneuvering, enhancing efficiency for heavier loads and larger operations. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, providing essential solutions for transporting goods within warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
