<p><strong>The Automotive Body Sealing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM</li><li>TPE</li><li>PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive body sealing system market comprises three main types: EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer), TPE (thermoplastic elastomer), and PVC (polyvinyl chloride). EPDM is known for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for exterior seals. TPE offers flexibility and recyclability, suitable for various applications, including interior sealing. PVC is cost-effective and widely used for door seals and window profiles, though less flexible. Each material serves distinct functions in enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and aesthetics.</p></p>
