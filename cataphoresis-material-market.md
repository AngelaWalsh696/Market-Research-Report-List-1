<p><strong>The Cataphoresis Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cathodic</li><li>Anodic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cataphoresis material market is classified into two main types: cathodic and anodic. Cathodic cataphoresis involves the movement of negatively charged particles towards the anode, often used for coatings that provide strong corrosion resistance and superior adhesion. Anodic cataphoresis, conversely, involves positively charged particles moving towards the cathode, typically yielding a more durable finish with enhanced resistance to chemical damage. Both types serve various industries, including automotive and electronics, for protective and aesthetic surface treatments.</p></p>
