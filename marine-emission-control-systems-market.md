<p><p>The Marine Emission Control Systems market comprises several technologies designed to reduce pollutants from ships. Scrubbers cleanse exhaust gases by removing sulfur oxides, while Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Systems recirculate a portion of exhaust back into the engine to lower nitrogen oxide emissions. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Systems utilize catalysts and additives to convert harmful nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water. Other systems may include various innovative approaches or combinations aimed at enhancing marine emissions management and compliance.</p></p>
