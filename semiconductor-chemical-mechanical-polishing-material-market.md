<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chemical Mechanical Polishing Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Slurry</li><li>CMP Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Material market is primarily divided into two types: CMP slurry and CMP pads. CMP slurry consists of abrasive particles suspended in a liquid, used to polish semiconductor wafers by removing excess material and achieving a smooth surface. CMP pads are engineered materials that provide a surface for the slurry to act upon during the polishing process, facilitating the effective interaction between the wafer and the abrasive particles. Both components are essential for the semiconductor fabrication process.</p></p>
