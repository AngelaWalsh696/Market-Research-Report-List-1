<p><ul><li>Side Entry</li><li>Rear Entry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheelchair accessible vans market is primarily divided into two types: side entry and rear entry. Side entry vans feature a ramp that extends from the side, allowing easy access in tight spaces and facilitating entry for passengers in wheelchairs. Rear entry vans, on the other hand, have a ramp that deploys from the back, offering a more spacious interior but requiring additional space for maneuvering when loading. Both types cater to different user needs, preferences, and mobility challenges.</p></p>
