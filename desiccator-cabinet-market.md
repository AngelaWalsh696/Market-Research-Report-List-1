<p><strong>The Desiccator Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum</li><li>Non-Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desiccator cabinet market is segmented into vacuum and non-vacuum types. Vacuum desiccator cabinets create a low-pressure environment, effectively removing moisture and maintaining a dry atmosphere for sensitive materials, making them ideal for laboratories and research facilities. In contrast, non-vacuum desiccator cabinets use desiccants to absorb moisture without altering the atmospheric pressure, suitable for applications that require consistent humidity control without specialized equipment. Both types cater to different needs in moisture-sensitive environments like pharmaceuticals and electronics.</p></p>
