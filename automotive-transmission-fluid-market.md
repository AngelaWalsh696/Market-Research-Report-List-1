<p><p>The automotive transmission fluid market comprises three main types: mineral, synthetic, and semi-synthetic fluids. Mineral fluids are derived from refined petroleum and offer basic protection and performance, making them cost-effective for standard vehicles. Synthetic fluids are chemically engineered for superior performance, providing better temperature stability and friction properties, ideal for high-performance or extreme conditions. Semi-synthetic fluids blend mineral and synthetic components, offering a balance of performance and affordability, catering to a wide range of automotive needs.</p></p>
