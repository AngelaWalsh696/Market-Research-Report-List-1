<p><p>The digital veterinary thermometers market comprises primarily two types: anal and ear thermometers. Anal thermometers measure body temperature via the rectum, offering high accuracy and reliability, especially in smaller animals. Ear thermometers use infrared technology to gauge temperature through the ear canal, providing quick and non-invasive readings. Both types cater to different veterinary needs, with anal thermometers often preferred for precise measurements, while ear thermometers are valued for their ease of use and speed, enhancing veterinary care efficiency.</p></p>
