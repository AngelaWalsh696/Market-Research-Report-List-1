<p><ul><li>EP Gear Oil</li><li>Synthetic EP Gear Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial gear oil market primarily features two main types: EP (Extreme Pressure) gear oil and synthetic EP gear oil. EP gear oil is formulated with additives that enhance performance under high pressures, providing essential lubrication for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, synthetic EP gear oil is composed of chemically engineered base oils that offer superior stability, reduced friction, and enhanced thermal properties, making it ideal for demanding environments. Both types are crucial for ensuring optimal gear performance and longevity in various industrial applications.</p></p>
