<p><ul><li>Rotary Heat Exchangers</li><li>Plate Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV) units are divided into two primary market types: rotary heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Rotary heat exchangers utilize a rotating wheel to transfer heat between incoming and outgoing air streams, maximizing thermal efficiency. In contrast, plate heat exchangers consist of stacked plates that allow heat transfer through conduction, enhancing airflow without mixing air streams. Both systems are designed to improve energy efficiency and indoor air quality in commercial buildings while reducing heating and cooling costs.</p></p>
