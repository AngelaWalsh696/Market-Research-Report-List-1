<p><ul><li>Single-Side</li><li>Two-Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Slot-die coating machines operate primarily in two market types: single-side and two-side coating. Single-side machines apply coatings to one surface of a substrate, ideal for products like flexible electronics and medical devices where one-sided application is sufficient. In contrast, two-side machines coat both surfaces simultaneously, catering to applications requiring uniformity on both sides, such as in battery production and advanced materials. The choice between these types depends on the specific application requirements and production efficiency needed.</p></p>
