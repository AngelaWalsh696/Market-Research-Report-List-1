<p><p>Amine additives in paints and coatings serve as crucial components to enhance performance characteristics. AMP (Amino Methyl Propanol) acts as a pH adjuster and stabilizer, improving film formation. DMAMP (Dimethyl Amino Methyl Propanol) enhances compatibility and reduces viscosity. DMAE (Dimethyl Amino Ethanol) is used for its emulsifying properties, while DEAE (Diethyl Amino Ethanol) improves flow and leveling in formulations. Other amine additives also contribute to specific functionalities, catering to diverse applications in the coatings industry.</p></p>
