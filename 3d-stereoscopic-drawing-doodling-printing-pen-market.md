<p><p>The 3D stereoscopic drawing doodling printing pen market features two primary types: Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and Stereolithography (SLA). FDM printing pens extrude thermoplastic materials, allowing users to create durable and complex designs through layer-by-layer deposition. In contrast, SLA printing pens utilize a light-sensitive resin, curing it layer by layer with UV light, resulting in high-resolution, intricate details. Both types cater to artists and designers seeking innovative ways to bring their imaginative concepts to life in three-dimensional form.</p></p>
