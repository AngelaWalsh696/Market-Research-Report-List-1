<p><p>The trimer acid market consists of three main types: standard trimer acid, distilled trimer acid, and hydrogenated trimer acid. Standard trimer acid is derived from the dimerization of unsaturated fatty acids and is commonly used in industrial applications. Distilled trimer acid undergoes further purification to enhance its quality and performance characteristics. Hydrogenated trimer acid results from the hydrogenation process, improving stability and reducing odor, making it ideal for high-end applications such as lubricants and coatings.</p></p>
