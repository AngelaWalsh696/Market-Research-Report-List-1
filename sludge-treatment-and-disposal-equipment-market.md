<p><p>The sludge treatment and disposal equipment market includes various technologies designed to manage wastewater sludge effectively. Screw presses utilize mechanical squeezing to dewater sludge, reducing its volume. Centrifuges use high-speed rotation to separate solids from liquids, offering efficient separation. Belt filter presses work by passing sludge between porous belts, allowing water to drain away. Other technologies may include rotary drum thickeners and hydro-extractors, providing additional options for effective sludge management in various industrial and municipal applications.</p></p>
