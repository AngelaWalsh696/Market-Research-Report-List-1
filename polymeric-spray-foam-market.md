<p><strong>The Polymeric Spray Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Cell Foam</li><li>Closed-Cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric spray foam market comprises two primary types: open-cell foam and closed-cell foam. Open-cell foam is characterized by its lower density and higher permeability, making it ideal for soundproofing and thermal insulation in walls and ceilings. Conversely, closed-cell foam is denser and serves as a moisture barrier, providing superior insulation and structural integrity, suitable for roofing and exterior applications. Both types cater to diverse needs across industries, enhancing energy efficiency and environmental performance in building projects.</p></p>
