<p><p>The Vinyl Chloride Vinyl Acetate Copolymer Resin market is primarily segmented into binary and ternary copolymers. Binary copolymers consist of two monomer units, typically vinyl chloride and vinyl acetate, resulting in enhanced properties like flexibility and adhesion. Ternary copolymers, on the other hand, include an additional third monomer, which further modifies the resin's characteristics, improving thermal stability and impact resistance. Both types serve diverse applications across packaging, automotive, and construction industries, catering to specific performance requirements.</p></p>
