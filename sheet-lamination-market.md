<p><strong>The Sheet Lamination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM)</li><li>Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sheet lamination is a versatile additive manufacturing process that includes two main market types: Ultrasonic Additive Manufacturing (UAM) and Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM). UAM utilizes ultrasonic welding to bond metal sheets, enabling the creation of intricate metal components with minimal waste and enhanced mechanical properties. In contrast, LOM involves layering and cutting sheets of plastic or paper to build parts, allowing for rapid prototyping and complex geometries, but typically with less strength compared to UAM-produced items. Together, they cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
