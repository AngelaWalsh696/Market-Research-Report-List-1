<p><p>The Variable Cam Timing (VCT) system market is categorized into two main types: single and double VCT systems. A single VCT system adjusts the timing of either the intake or exhaust camshaft, optimizing performance and efficiency. In contrast, a double VCT system can modify both camshafts, providing enhanced control over engine parameters, leading to improved power output and fuel economy. These innovations cater to diverse engine designs, addressing varying demands for performance and emissions standards.</p></p>
