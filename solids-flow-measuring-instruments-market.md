<p><strong>The Solids Flow Measuring Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contacting Measuring Instruments</li><li>Contactless Measuring Instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solids flow measuring instruments market consists of two primary types: contacting and contactless measuring instruments. Contacting instruments physically interact with the material, providing precise flow measurements through methods such as load cells or rotary devices. In contrast, contactless instruments measure flow without direct contact, utilizing technologies like laser or microwave sensing, which reduces wear and contamination. Both types are essential for various industries, including food processing, pharmaceuticals, and bulk material handling, ensuring accurate flow monitoring and process optimization.</p></p>
