<p><p>The Building Mortar Market comprises Wet Mixed Mortar and Dry Mortar. Wet Mixed Mortar is pre-mixed with water, ready for immediate use, offering convenience and consistency but requiring quick application due to limited shelf life. Dry Mortar, a blend of dry ingredients like cement, sand, and additives, is mixed with water on-site, providing flexibility and longer storage. Both types cater to construction needs, with Wet Mixed Mortar favored for efficiency and Dry Mortar for adaptability and cost-effectiveness in diverse projects.</p></p>
