<p><strong>The Automotive Night Vision Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Automotive NVS</li><li>Passive Automotive NVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Night Vision Systems (NVS) market is categorized into active and passive systems. Active Automotive NVS utilizes infrared light sources to illuminate the road, enhancing visibility in low-light conditions by projecting light that is then captured by cameras. In contrast, Passive Automotive NVS relies on thermal imaging technologies to detect heat emitted from objects, vehicles, and pedestrians, providing valuable visual information without the need for additional light sources. Both systems aim to improve safety during nighttime driving.</p></p>
