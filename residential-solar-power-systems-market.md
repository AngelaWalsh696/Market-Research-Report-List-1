<p><strong>The Residential Solar Power Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-grid</li><li>Off-grid with Grid Support</li><li>Grid-tied</li><li>Grid-tied with Battery Back Up</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential solar power systems can be categorized into four main types:</p><p>1. **Off-grid** systems operate independently, using battery storage for power supply, ideal for remote locations without grid access.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Off-grid with grid support** combines off-grid functionality with a connection to the grid, providing backup options while reducing reliance on the grid.</p><p>3. **Grid-tied** systems connect directly to the grid, allowing for net metering and no battery storage, enhancing cost-effectiveness.</p><p>4. **Grid-tied with battery backup** provides both grid connectivity and battery storage, offering reliability during outages while leveraging grid benefits.</p></p>
