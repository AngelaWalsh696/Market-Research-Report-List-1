<p><ul><li>Electro Chemical</li><li>Thermal Storage</li><li>Mechanical Energy Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The energy storage system market encompasses three main types: electrochemical, thermal, and mechanical energy storage. Electrochemical storage primarily involves batteries, converting chemical energy into electrical energy for grid support and peak shaving. Thermal storage systems store energy as heat for later use, often in concentrated solar power or heating applications. Mechanical energy storage includes methods like pumped hydro or flywheels, utilizing kinetic or gravitational potential energy. Each type addresses unique needs in grid stability, renewable integration, and energy management.</p></p>
