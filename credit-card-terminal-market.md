<p><p>The credit card terminal market consists of three main types: countertop card machines, portable card machines, and mobile card machines. Countertop card machines are stationary devices typically used in retail settings, providing robust features for high-volume transactions. Portable card machines are wireless units that offer flexibility for businesses that require mobility, enabling transactions away from a fixed location. Mobile card machines utilize smartphones or tablets, allowing small businesses and freelancers to accept payments anywhere, enhancing convenience and accessibility.</p></p>
