<p><p>The aerial lift work truck market encompasses various types of equipment designed for elevated work tasks. Boom lifts, equipped with extendable arms, allow access to high or hard-to-reach areas, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor projects. Telehandlers combine lifting capabilities with versatility, functioning as both a forklift and a platform, which is useful in agriculture and construction for handling materials at height.</p></p>
