<p><p>The test gauges market is divided into two primary types: panel mount test gauges and stem mount test gauges. Panel mount test gauges are designed to be installed on surfaces, often enabling easy monitoring in industrial settings. They are commonly used in control panels and equipment dashboards. Conversely, stem mount test gauges are equipped with a stem that allows for flexible orientation and easier access to pressure readings in confined spaces. Both types serve essential functions in pressure measurement and monitoring applications.</p></p>
