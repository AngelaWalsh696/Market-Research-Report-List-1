<p><strong>The Blood Irradiators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiators market comprises two primary types: X-ray and gamma-ray blood irradiation systems. X-ray blood irradiation uses high-energy X-rays to sterilize blood products, reducing the risk of transfusion-related infections while preserving blood components. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma radiation, often from isotopes like Cobalt-60, to achieve similar sterilization effects. Both methods aim to enhance the safety of blood transfusions by inactivating pathogens while ensuring the integrity and efficacy of the blood products.</p></p>
