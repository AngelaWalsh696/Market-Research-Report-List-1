<p><p>The Fire Resistant Flexible Sealant Market is primarily categorized into two types: Elastometric Type and Intumescent Type. Elastometric sealants are designed to maintain flexibility while resisting fire, making them ideal for dynamic joint applications. In contrast, Intumescent sealants expand when exposed to heat, creating a protective barrier against flames and smoke. Both types are essential for construction and maintenance in fire safety, ensuring buildings can withstand fire exposure while providing effective sealing against moisture and air infiltration.</p></p>
