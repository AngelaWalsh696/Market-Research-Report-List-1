<p><p>T-slot cylinder sensors are specialized devices used in automation and industrial applications to detect the position of moving components. The market primarily comprises two types: PNP and NPN cylinder sensors. PNP sensors source current to the load, offering a positive output signal when activated, making them suitable for systems requiring a high side switch. Conversely, NPN sensors sink current, providing a negative output signal, ideal for low side switching applications. Both types enhance operational efficiency and precision in various machinery settings.</p></p>
