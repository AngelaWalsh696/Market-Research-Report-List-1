<p><ul><li>Weak Current</li><li>Strong Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plasma Arc Cutting market is segmented into weak current and strong current categories based on the intensity of the plasma arc used during cutting operations. Weak current plasma cutting is typically employed for thin materials and precision tasks, delivering lower heat and reducing the risk of warping. In contrast, strong current plasma cutting is utilized for thicker materials, providing faster cutting speeds and greater penetration. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various industry needs from metal fabrication to automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
