<p><p>The metal 3D printing solution market comprises several types, primarily Selective Laser Melting (SLM), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and other emerging technologies. SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powder layer by layer, enabling complex geometries and high precision. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, offering excellent material properties and potential for larger build sizes. Other methods include Direct Energy Deposition and binder jetting, which cater to specialized applications and materials.</p></p>
