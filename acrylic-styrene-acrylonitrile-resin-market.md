<p><p>The Acrylic Styrene Acrylonitrile (ASA) resin market is categorized into two main types: extrusion grade and injection grade. Extrusion grade ASA is primarily utilized for processes requiring continuous shapes, such as pipes and sheets, offering excellent weather resistance and surface finish. Injection grade ASA, on the other hand, is designed for moldable applications, providing superior strength and durability in produced parts. Both grades leverage ASA’s unique properties, including UV resistance and impact strength, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
