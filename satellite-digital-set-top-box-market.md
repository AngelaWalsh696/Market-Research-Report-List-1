<p><ul><li>OTT (Over the Top)</li><li>DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Satellite Digital Set-Top Box market comprises two distinct types: OTT (Over the Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT services deliver content directly over the internet, bypassing traditional cable or satellite systems, allowing users to access streaming platforms seamlessly. In contrast, DTT refers to the transmission of digital television signals directly to homes using terrestrial antennas, providing viewers with access to broadcast channels without requiring satellite dishes or internet connectivity. Both cater to diverse consumer preferences for content delivery.</p></p>
