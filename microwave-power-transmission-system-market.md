<p><p>Microwave Power Transmission (MPT) systems utilize different technologies to transmit energy wirelessly. Induction systems leverage electromagnetic fields to transfer power between coils, suitable for short-range applications. Magnetic Resonance systems, on the other hand, use resonant coupling between two tuned circuits, allowing efficient energy transfer over longer distances. Both methods offer unique advantages: induction is often simpler and cost-effective for small devices, while magnetic resonance provides flexibility and efficiency for larger-scale energy needs, such as powering vehicles or remote sensors.</p></p>
