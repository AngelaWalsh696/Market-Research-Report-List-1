<p><strong>The Turboprop Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial-Flow Type Turboprop Engines</li><li>Centrifugal Type Turboprop Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turboprop engine market is primarily divided into two types: axial-flow and centrifugal turboprop engines. Axial-flow turboprop engines feature a design where air flows parallel to the axis of the engine, often resulting in higher efficiency and performance, especially at greater speeds. Conversely, centrifugal turboprop engines utilize a design where air is drawn in and then expelled radially, leading to greater thrust at lower speeds. Both types cater to different aviation needs, impacting aircraft design and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
