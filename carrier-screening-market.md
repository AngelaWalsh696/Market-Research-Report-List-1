<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Carrier Screening Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molecular Screening Test</li><li>Biochemical Screening Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carrier screening market is categorized primarily into two types: molecular screening tests and biochemical screening tests. Molecular screening tests detect specific genetic mutations associated with inherited disorders, utilizing techniques like DNA sequencing. In contrast, biochemical screening tests analyze metabolic byproducts in blood or urine to identify carriers of conditions such as cystic fibrosis or phenylketonuria. Both types facilitate early identification of genetic risks in individuals and couples planning families, enhancing informed reproductive choices and promoting better health outcomes.</p></p>
