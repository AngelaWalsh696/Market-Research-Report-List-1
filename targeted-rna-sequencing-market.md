<p><p>Targeted RNA sequencing encompasses several market types, including exome sequencing, enrichment sequencing, and amplicon sequencing. Exome sequencing focuses on capturing and sequencing the protein-coding regions of the genome, providing insights into genetic variations. Enrichment sequencing targets specific RNA molecules or pathways, enhancing the detection of low-abundance transcripts. Amplicon sequencing involves PCR amplification of specific regions, enabling detailed analysis of targeted genes or variations, often used in oncology and personalized medicine for precise diagnostics and treatment strategies.</p></p>
