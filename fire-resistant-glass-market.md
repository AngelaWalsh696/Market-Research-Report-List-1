<p><p>The fire-resistant glass market includes several types, each designed for specific applications. Laminated fire-resistant glass consists of multiple layers, offering both impact resistance and fire protection. Tempered fire-resistant glass is heat-treated for strength and can endure high temperatures. Wired fire-resistant glass incorporates a wire mesh that holds the glass together during a fire, providing stability. Ceramic fire-resistant glass is made from advanced ceramics, offering superior thermal insulation and clarity, making it suitable for high-risk environments.</p></p>
