<p><ul><li>Underlayment</li><li>Toppings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-leveling concrete market is primarily divided into two types: underlayment and toppings. Underlayment is designed to create a smooth, flat base for flooring installations, helping to hide imperfections and providing an even surface for subsequent flooring materials. Toppings, on the other hand, are used to enhance the surface aesthetics and functionality, often incorporating decorative finishes or colors. Both types are extensively employed in residential and commercial applications for their durability, ease of application, and quick-setting properties.</p></p>
