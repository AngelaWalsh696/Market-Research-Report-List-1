<p><strong>The RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active RFID Antennas</li><li>Passive RFID Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID antennas market is divided into two main types: active and passive RFID antennas. Active RFID antennas are used with tags that have an onboard power source, enabling longer read ranges and faster data transfer rates, ideal for real-time tracking in logistics and asset management. In contrast, passive RFID antennas work with tags that derive power from the reader's signal, making them cost-effective and suitable for inventory control and supply chain applications, albeit with shorter read distances.</p></p>
