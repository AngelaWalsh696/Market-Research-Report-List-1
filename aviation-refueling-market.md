<p><ul><li>Mobile Tankers</li><li>Hydrant Dispenser</li><li>Fueling Cabinets/Skids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation refueling market comprises three primary types: Mobile Tankers, Hydrant Dispensers, and Fueling Cabinets/Skids. Mobile Tankers are versatile vehicles that deliver fuel directly to aircraft on the tarmac, offering flexibility in various locations. Hydrant Dispensers are fixed systems integrated into airport infrastructure, allowing for efficient fueling via underground pipelines. Fueling Cabinets/Skids are compact, portable units designed for easy transport and storage of aviation fuel, ideal for smaller airports or remote locations. Each type serves unique operational needs in the aviation industry.</p></p>
