<p><p>The bio-based rubber processing oil market is categorized into three main types: aromatic, naphthenic, and paraffinic. Aromatic oils, derived from plant sources, provide excellent performance in enhancing elasticity and thermal stability. Naphthenic oils, noted for their flexibility and low volatility, are ideal for high-performance applications. Paraffinic oils, with their high viscosity and light color, are preferred for their consistency and ease of handling. Each type serves distinct applications in the rubber industry, focusing on sustainability and performance.</p></p>
