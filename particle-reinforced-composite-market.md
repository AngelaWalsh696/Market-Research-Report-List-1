<p><p>The particle reinforced composite market encompasses various types based on the reinforcing particles used in metal matrices. SiC/Al composites utilize silicon carbide to enhance strength and wear resistance in aluminum matrices. B/Al composites leverage boron for improved toughness and thermal properties. BC/Al combines boron carbide with aluminum for superior hardness. Al2O3/Al composites incorporate aluminum oxide for enhanced thermal stability and corrosion resistance. Other categories may include alternative materials or specific applications, broadening the versatility of metal matrix composites in various industries.</p></p>
