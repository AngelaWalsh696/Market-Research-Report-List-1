<p><ul><li>Conventional Fire Detection and Alarm Systems</li><li>Addressable Fire Detection and Alarm Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire alarm and detection systems market consists of two primary types: conventional and addressable systems. Conventional fire detection systems are simpler and use a series of zones, activating alarms based on the detection of smoke or heat in specific areas. In contrast, addressable fire detection systems provide more sophisticated monitoring, allowing individual devices to report precisely where an alarm is triggered, improving response times and reducing false alarms. This enhanced communication leads to more effective fire safety management.</p></p>
