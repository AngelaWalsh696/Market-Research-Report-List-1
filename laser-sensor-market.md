<p><p>The laser sensor market comprises various types, primarily focusing on digital laser sensors and CMOS laser sensors. Digital laser sensors utilize advanced digital processing for precise distance measurement and enhanced accuracy, making them ideal for industrial applications. On the other hand, CMOS laser sensors leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, which allows for miniaturization and integration into compact devices, offering high-speed performance and lower power consumption. Both types cater to a wide range of sectors, including manufacturing, automation, and robotics.</p></p>
