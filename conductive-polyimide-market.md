<p><p>Conductive polyimide is a specialized polymer material that combines the high thermal stability, mechanical strength, and chemical resistance of traditional polyimide with electrical conductivity. This is achieved by incorporating conductive fillers such as carbon nanotubes, graphene, or metal particles into the polyimide matrix. It is widely used in electronics, aerospace, automotive, and medical industries for applications like flexible circuits, sensors, EMI shielding, and thermal management.</p><p>The Conductive Polyimide Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period.
