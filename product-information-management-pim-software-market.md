<p><p>Product Information Management (PIM) software can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise PIM involves installing the software on local servers, providing businesses with greater control over their data and customization options. In contrast, cloud-based PIM offers a subscription model where data is hosted remotely, allowing for easier scalability, automatic updates, and remote access. Both types aim to streamline product data management and enhance collaboration across teams, but they cater to different business needs and infrastructure preferences.</p></p>
