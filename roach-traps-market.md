<p><ul><li>Bait Stations</li><li>Granular Baits</li><li>Gels Baits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roach traps market is categorized into three main types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are enclosed traps filled with attractants to lure roaches, ensuring safety for pets and children. Granular baits consist of powdery substances that can be scattered in roach-infested areas, appealing to roaches through their food-like appearance. Gel baits, in contrast, are viscous substances applied in specific spots, effectively targeting roaches while reducing exposure and enhancing efficiency in pest control.</p></p>
