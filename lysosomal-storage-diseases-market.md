<p><p>Lysosomal Storage Diseases (LSDs) involve enzyme deficiencies leading to the accumulation of toxic substrates. Market types include Enzyme Replacement Therapy (ERT), where missing enzymes are supplied to reduce substrate buildup; Substrate Reduction Therapy (SRT), which limits substrate production to decrease accumulation; and Cystine Depleting Agents, specifically targeting cystine levels in cystinosis. Each approach aims to manage symptoms and improve quality of life for patients, addressing different mechanisms of disease progression and offering tailored treatment solutions.</p></p>
