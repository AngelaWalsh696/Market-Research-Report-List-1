Bulk Emulsions are water-resistant explosives ideal for wet conditions, whereas Packaged Emulsions are pre-packaged for ease of handling. Initiation Accessories include detonators and fuses that ignite explosives, playing a crucial role in ensuring precise and safe detonation in various applications.</p></p>
