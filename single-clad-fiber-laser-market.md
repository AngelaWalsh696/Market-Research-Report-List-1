<p><p>The single-clad fiber laser market is primarily categorized into Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed fiber lasers. CW fiber lasers deliver a constant beam of light, ideal for applications requiring steady output, such as cutting and drilling. In contrast, pulsed fiber lasers generate high-energy bursts of light, making them suitable for tasks that require precision, like marking and engraving. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing efficiency in various manufacturing processes and applications in telecommunications and medical devices.</p></p>
