<p><p>The Lithium Battery Fuel Gauge IC market is categorized into two main types: Single-Cell and Multi-Cell ICs. Single-Cell fuel gauge ICs are designed for individual lithium battery applications, providing precise voltage and charge monitoring for devices like smartphones and wearables. In contrast, Multi-Cell fuel gauge ICs manage multiple battery cells in series, commonly used in electric vehicles and energy storage systems. These ICs enhance performance, safety, and longevity of battery systems by providing accurate state-of-charge information across multiple cells.</p></p>
