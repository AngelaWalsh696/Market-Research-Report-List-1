<p><p>The enteral nutritional products market encompasses various formulations designed for individuals with specific dietary needs, particularly those unable to consume regular food. Enteral nutritional powders are concentrated forms that are reconstituted with water, offering flexibility in nutrient intake. Enteral nutritional emulsions combine fats, carbohydrates, and proteins into a stable mixture for easy absorption, often used in tube feeding. Enteral nutritional suspensions are liquid formulations with suspended particles, providing a balanced nutrient profile, suitable for individuals requiring easier passage through feeding tubes.</p></p>
