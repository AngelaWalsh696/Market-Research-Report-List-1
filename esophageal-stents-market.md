<p><strong>The Esophageal Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-expandable Metallic Stents (SEMS)</li><li>Self-expanding Plastic Stents (SEPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The esophageal stents market features primarily two types: self-expandable metallic stents (SEMS) and self-expanding plastic stents (SEPS). SEMS are made of metal and provide durability and support for patients with esophageal obstructions, often used in conditions like cancer. SEPS, on the other hand, are made of plastic, offering a flexible and lower-cost alternative, typically used for short-term applications. Both types aim to alleviate dysphagia and improve patient quality of life by maintaining esophageal patency.</p></p>
