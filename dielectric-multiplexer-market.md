<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dielectric Multiplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEM Mode</li><li>TE Mode</li><li>TM Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dielectric Multiplexer market is categorized into three primary modes: TEM (Transverse Electric and Magnetic), TE (Transverse Electric), and TM (Transverse Magnetic). TEM mode supports both electric and magnetic fields perpendicular to the direction of propagation, making it suitable for broadband applications. TE mode has only electric fields transverse to the propagation direction, ideal for high-frequency applications. TM mode features only magnetic fields transverse to the propagation direction, often used in applications requiring specific polarization. Each mode caters to distinct performance needs and application areas.</p></p>
