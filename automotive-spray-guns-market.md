<p><p>The automotive spray guns market features several types, including High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) and Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) guns. HVLP guns use high air volume at low pressure to minimize paint waste and achieve fine finishes, making them popular for automotive applications. LVLP guns operate at even lower pressure and air volume, allowing for more precise control and reduced overspray. Other types include airless and electrostatic guns, each catering to specific painting needs and techniques in the automotive industry.</p></p>
