<p><p>The firestop material market is primarily categorized into organic and inorganic fireproof materials. Organic fireproof materials, derived from natural sources, often include substances like cellulose and certain polymers, offering fire resistance while being environmentally friendly. In contrast, inorganic fireproof materials, such as mineral wool and gypsum board, are made from non-combustible minerals, providing superior thermal protection and structural integrity. Both types are essential for enhancing safety in construction by preventing the spread of fire and smoke between compartments.</p></p>
