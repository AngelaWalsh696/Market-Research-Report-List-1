<p><strong>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling</li><li>Feedback ANC</li><li>Hybrid ANC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) earphone market includes three main types: Feedforward ANC, Feedback ANC, and Hybrid ANC. </p><p>Feedforward ANC uses external microphones to detect ambient noise and generate counteracting sound waves, making it effective for consistent sounds. Feedback ANC utilizes internal microphones to monitor sound within the earcup, adjusting cancellation dynamically, which is suitable for varying noise environments. Hybrid ANC combines both methods, offering a comprehensive solution for diverse noise types, ensuring an optimal listening experience across different settings.</p></p>
