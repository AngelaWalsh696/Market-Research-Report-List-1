<p><p>The fixed panoramic sunroof market is primarily categorized into two types: laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers fused together, providing enhanced safety by reducing shattering and improving insulation. It also offers superior UV protection. Tempered glass, on the other hand, undergoes a heat treatment process that increases its strength and makes it more resistant to impacts. Both types enhance vehicle aesthetics while providing natural light and an open-air feel, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
