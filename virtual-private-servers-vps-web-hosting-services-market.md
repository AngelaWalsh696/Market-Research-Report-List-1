<p><p>Virtual Private Servers (VPS) web hosting services come in two primary market types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based VPS offers scalability and flexibility, utilizing multiple servers to distribute resources, ensuring high availability and redundancy. This model is ideal for businesses with fluctuating demands. In contrast, web-based VPS relies on a single physical server, providing dedicated resources for clients.
