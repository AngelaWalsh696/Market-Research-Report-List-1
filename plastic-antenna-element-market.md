<p><p>The plastic antenna element market is primarily categorized into two types: LDS (Laser Direct Structuring) and LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) as well as Modified PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide). LDS allows for precise antenna design on 3D structures, enhancing performance and integration. LCP offers excellent thermal stability and low moisture absorption, making it suitable for high-frequency applications. Modified PPS combines chemical resistance and mechanical strength, offering durability in diverse environments. Each type is tailored for specific applications in the telecommunications and automotive industries.</p></p>
