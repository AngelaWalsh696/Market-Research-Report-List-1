Cutting and grinding lubricants minimize friction and wear during machining operations, improving tool life and surface finish. Corrosion preventives protect metal surfaces from rust and degradation, extending the lifespan of machinery and components. Together, these lubricants play a vital role in enhancing efficiency and product quality in metalworking processes.</p></p>
