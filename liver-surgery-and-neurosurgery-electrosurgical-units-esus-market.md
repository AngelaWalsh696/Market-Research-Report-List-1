<p><p>The market for liver surgery and neurosurgery electrosurgical units (ESUs) includes various types of devices designed for surgical precision and efficiency. Monopole electrosurgical units utilize a single active electrode to create a high-frequency current for cutting and coagulation. Bipolar electrosurgical units have two electrodes, allowing for more controlled tissue effects with minimized collateral damage. Vessel sealing units are specialized devices that effectively seal blood vessels during surgery, enhancing safety and reducing procedure times. Together, these technologies significantly improve surgical outcomes.</p></p>
