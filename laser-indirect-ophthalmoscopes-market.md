<p><strong>The Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photocoagulating Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscope</li><li>Photo Disrupting Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscope</li><li>Photo Ablating Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscopes market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Photocoagulating Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscopes** are used for retinal photocoagulation, treating conditions like diabetic retinopathy by creating controlled burns to seal leaking blood vessels. </p><p>2. **Photo Disrupting Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscopes** focus on breaking down tissue, useful for procedures such as cataract surgery or addressing vitreous detachment. </p><p>3. **Photo Ablating Laser Indirect Ophthalmoscopes** remove or vaporize tissue, applied in treating ocular tumors or advanced retinal disease, enhancing precision in delicate eye surgeries.</p></p>
