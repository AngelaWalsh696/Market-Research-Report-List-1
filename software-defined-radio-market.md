<p><p>Software Defined Radio (SDR) refers to a communication system where traditional hardware components are replaced by software algorithms that perform the same functions. This approach offers greater flexibility, adaptability, and functionality, enabling devices to handle various communication protocols without the need for reconfiguration of hardware. As a result, SDR is widely used in military, commercial, and amateur radio applications.</p><p>The Software Defined Radio Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by advancements in wireless communication technologies, the increasing demand for bandwidth, and the need for multi-standard communication systems. The military sector continues to be a major contributor, leveraging SDR for secure and efficient communication.
