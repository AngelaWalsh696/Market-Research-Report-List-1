<p><strong>The Specialty Fiber Optic Cables for Electric Utilities and Communications Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OPGW</li><li>OPPC</li><li>ADSS</li><li>OPLC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty fiber optic cables for electric utilities and communications include various types designed for specific applications. OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) integrates fiber optics with overhead power lines, providing both grounding and communication. OPPC (Optical Power Composite Cable) combines electrical conductors with fiber, suited for new installations. ADSS (All-Dielectric Self-Supporting) cables are used for aerial installations without metallic support, ideal for non-conductive environments. OPLC (Optical Power Line Communications) cables enable data transmission alongside power distribution, enhancing network capabilities.</p></p>
