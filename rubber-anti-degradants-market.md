<p><ul><li>Amine Antidegradants</li><li>Phenolic Antidegradants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber anti-degradants market is primarily categorized into two main types: amine and phenolic anti-degradants. Amine anti-degradants are used to protect rubber from aging and deterioration, offering excellent resistance to heat and sunlight. They are crucial for enhancing the longevity of rubber products. In contrast, phenolic anti-degradants provide superior protection against oxidative degradation, making them suitable for applications requiring enhanced thermal stability. Together, these anti-degradants play a vital role in extending the lifespan and performance of rubber materials.</p></p>
