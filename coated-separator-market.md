<p><p>The coated separator market comprises various types, primarily including PVDF coated separators and ceramic coated separators. PVDF coated separators utilize polyvinylidene fluoride for enhanced chemical resistance and thermal stability, improving the overall efficiency and lifespan of batteries. On the other hand, ceramic coated separators incorporate a ceramic layer, providing excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength, which enhances safety and performance. Both types serve critical roles in energy storage applications, particularly in lithium-ion batteries, catering to the demands of high-performance energy systems.</p></p>
