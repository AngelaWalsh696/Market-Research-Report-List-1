<p><strong>The Polymer Modifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Performance-tailored Tougheners</li><li>Coupling Agents</li><li>Flexibilizers</li><li>Mixed plastics Compatibilizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymer modifiers market encompasses various types that enhance the properties of polymers. Performance-tailored tougheners improve impact resistance and durability. Coupling agents enhance adhesion between dissimilar materials, ensuring better performance. Flexibilizers increase the elasticity and flexibility of rigid plastics, allowing for diverse applications. Mixed plastics compatibilizers facilitate the blending of incompatible polymers, promoting uniformity and stability in composite materials. Together, these modifiers optimize polymer performance for specific applications across industries like automotive, packaging, and construction.</p></p>
