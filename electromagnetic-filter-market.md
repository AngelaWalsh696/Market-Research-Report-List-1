<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The electromagnetic filter market is categorized into several types based on their frequency response characteristics. Low-pass filters allow signals below a certain cutoff frequency to pass through while attenuating higher frequencies. High-pass filters do the opposite, enabling signals above a specific frequency to transmit while blocking lower frequencies. Band-pass filters permit a specific range of frequencies to pass, rejecting those outside this range. Additionally, other filters like band-stop and notch filters serve niche applications, further expanding the market's diversity.</p></p>
