<p><p>Adventitious agent testing is essential in the biopharmaceutical industry to ensure product safety. The market is divided into two key types: specific and non-specific methods. Specific methods target known pathogens, utilizing techniques like PCR and cell culture assays for precise identification. In contrast, non-specific methods evaluate the overall sterility and integrity of a product, employing broader assays like mycoplasma tests or general sterility tests. Both approaches play a critical role in safeguarding against unexpected contaminants in therapeutic products.</p></p>
