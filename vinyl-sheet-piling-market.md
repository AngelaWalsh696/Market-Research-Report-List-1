<p><strong>The Vinyl Sheet Piling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VZ Type</li><li>VU Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vinyl sheet piling is available in two primary market types: VZ and VU. The VZ type features interlocking planks with a Z-shaped design, providing enhanced strength and resistance to lateral forces, making it ideal for seawalls and bulkheads. The VU type has a U-shaped design that offers flexibility and ease of installation, commonly used in applications like retaining walls and construction projects. Both types are favored for their durability, corrosion resistance, and eco-friendly properties, fulfilling diverse engineering needs.</p></p>
