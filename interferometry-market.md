<p><p>The interferometry market encompasses various types of interferometers, each with unique applications. Laser Fizeau interferometers measure surface flatness and optical component quality. Mach-Zehnder interferometers analyze phase shifts in light for precise measurements. Michelson interferometers are versatile in spectroscopy and optical testing. Fabry-Perot interferometers enhance spectral resolution in lasers. Sagnac interferometers are crucial in gyroscope technology for rotation sensing. Twyman-Green interferometers are used for testing optics and surface imperfections, showcasing a diverse range of applications across scientific and industrial sectors.</p></p>
