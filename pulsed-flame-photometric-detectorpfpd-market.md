<p><p>The Pulsed Flame Photometric Detector (PFPD) is an analytical instrument used primarily in gas chromatography for the detection of trace amounts of sulfur and phosphorus compounds in various samples. This sensitivity to sulfur and phosphorus makes PFPD particularly valuable in industries such as petroleum, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p><p>The market for PFPD is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for sophisticated analytical techniques in various sectors, including pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and petrochemicals.
