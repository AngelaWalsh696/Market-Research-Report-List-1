<p><p>The natural surgical glue market encompasses three primary types: fibrin, collagen, and gelatin. Fibrin glue is designed to facilitate wound healing by mimicking natural clotting processes, providing an effective sealant. Collagen glue utilizes collagen, a key protein in tissue structure, to promote adhesion and healing. Gelatin glue, derived from animal collagen, serves as a biocompatible adhesive that supports tissue regeneration. Each type plays a crucial role in surgical procedures, enhancing recovery and reducing complications.</p></p>
