<p><p>The Earthquake Early Warning (EEW) System market comprises three primary components: earthquake sensors, servers, and receivers. Earthquake sensors detect seismic activity and provide real-time data on tremors. Servers process and analyze this data, assessing the earthquake's magnitude and potential impact. Receivers, typically in the form of alerts sent to users or automated systems, deliver timely notifications to individuals and organizations, enabling them to take protective measures before shaking occurs. Together, these components enhance safety and preparedness during seismic events.</p></p>
