<p><p>Mineral grease is a lubricant made from mineral oil and thickeners, used to reduce friction and protect machinery. Calcium-based grease offers water resistance and is ideal for low-speed applications. Lithium-based grease is versatile, with high-temperature stability and mechanical stability, suitable for automotive and industrial uses. Aluminum complex grease provides excellent water resistance and high-temperature performance, often used in marine and steel industries. Barium complex grease offers superior corrosion protection and high load-carrying capacity, making it suitable for extreme conditions and heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific operational needs.</p></p>
