<p><p>The medical plastic packaging market comprises several key materials, each suited for specific applications. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) is known for its durability and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for bottles and containers. Polypropylene (PP) offers excellent clarity and heat resistance, often used for syringes and medical devices. Polycarbonate (PC) is valued for its strength and transparency, suitable for surgical trays. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is lightweight and recyclable, commonly used for pre-fillable syringes and IV bags. Other materials include PVC and ABS, catering to niche requirements in medical packaging.</p></p>
