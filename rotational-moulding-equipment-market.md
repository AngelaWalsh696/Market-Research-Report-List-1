<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rotational Moulding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RockandRollMachines</li><li>ShuttleMachines</li><li>CarouselMachines</li><li>ClamshellMachines</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotational moulding equipment includes various types designed for producing hollow plastic products. **Rock and Roll Machines** tilt and rotate molds simultaneously, ideal for elongated items. **Shuttle Machines** use a single arm to move molds between heating and cooling stations, suitable for small-scale production. **Carousel Machines** feature multiple arms rotating molds through heating, cooling, and loading stations, enabling high-volume output. **Clamshell Machines** open and close like a clamshell, offering efficient heating and cooling for medium-sized products. **Others** include specialized or hybrid systems catering to unique production needs. Each type optimizes efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
