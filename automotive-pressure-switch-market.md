<p><strong>The Automotive Pressure Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Function Pressure Switch</li><li>HPCO</li><li>Fan</li><li>Compressor Cycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Pressure Switch market includes various types designed for specific functions. **Dual Function Pressure Switches** combine multiple operations, such as monitoring and controlling pressure levels in a single unit. **HPCO (High-Pressure Cut-Out) Switches** protect systems by shutting off components when pressure exceeds safe limits. **Fan Pressure Switches** regulate cooling fan operation based on system pressure. **Compressor Cycling Switches** control the on/off cycling of compressors to maintain optimal pressure in air conditioning systems. Each type ensures efficient and safe operation of automotive systems.</p></p>
