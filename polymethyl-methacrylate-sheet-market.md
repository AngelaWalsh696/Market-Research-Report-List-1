<p><ul><li>Extruded</li><li>Cast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA) sheet market is primarily divided into extruded and cast types. Extruded PMMA sheets are produced by forcing molten polymer through a die, resulting in a continuous sheet that is cost-effective and widely used for signage and glazing. In contrast, cast PMMA sheets are made by pouring liquid polymer into molds, offering superior optical clarity and customizable thickness, making them ideal for applications like high-end displays and protective barriers. Both types serve distinct industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
