<p><p>The biomass transformer oil market consists of two primary types: natural transformer fluid and synthetic transformer oil. Natural transformer fluids are derived from renewable sources such as vegetable oils, offering biodegradable properties and lower environmental impact. Conversely, synthetic transformer oils are engineered from chemical processes for enhanced thermal stability and performance. Both types are used in transformers for improved efficiency and reduced ecological footprints, catering to the growing demand for sustainable energy solutions in the electrical industry.</p></p>
