<p><strong>The Automotive ToF Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Direct"</li><li>"Indirect"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Time-of-Flight (ToF) camera market is divided into two main types: direct and indirect. Direct ToF cameras measure distance by emitting a light pulse and calculating the time taken for the reflection to return, providing real-time depth perception. Indirect ToF cameras, on the other hand, rely on phase shift measurements of modulated light to infer distance, typically offering improved accuracy over varying lighting conditions. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and enabling advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
