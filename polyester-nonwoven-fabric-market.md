<p><strong>The Polyester Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond Method</li><li>Spunlace Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester nonwoven fabric market features several key manufacturing methods. The spunbond method involves the continuous bonding of extruded polyester fibers, producing durable and lightweight fabrics ideal for applications like hygiene products and geotextiles. The spunlace method, on the other hand, uses high-pressure water jets to intertwine fibers, yielding softer and more absorbent materials, often used in wipes and medical applications. Additionally, other methods include needle punching and thermal bonding, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs within the nonwoven sector.</p></p>
