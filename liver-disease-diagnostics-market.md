<p><p>The liver disease diagnostics market encompasses various types of tests used to identify liver conditions. Imaging tests, such as ultrasound, CT scans, and MRI, provide visual evaluations of liver structure and abnormalities. Blood tests measure liver enzymes, proteins, and bilirubin levels to assess liver function and detect damage. Biopsy involves sampling liver tissue for microscopic analysis to confirm diagnosis and stage disease severity. Other diagnostic methods may include non-invasive tests, such as elastography, to evaluate liver stiffness and fibrosis.</p></p>
