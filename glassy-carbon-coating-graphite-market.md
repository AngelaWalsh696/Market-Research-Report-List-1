<p><p>The glassy carbon coating graphite market encompasses various types of coatings, primarily focusing on VGI (Vacuum Glazing and Injection) and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) methods. VGI involves creating a vacuum-sealed barrier with injected materials, enhancing thermal insulation and durability. In contrast, CVD is a chemical process that deposits thin films onto surfaces, promoting uniform coating and improved chemical resistance. Both techniques are essential in various industrial applications, enhancing the performance and longevity of graphite materials.</p></p>
